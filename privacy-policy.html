Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Rewards Easily ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

When you use Rewards Easily as a guest, we do not collect any personal information. If you create an account, we collect:

  • Your email address and encrypted password
  • The list of credit cards you add to the app
  • Your in-app preferences (e.g. dark mode, biometric settings)

Advertising

Rewards Easily displays ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. You can learn more about Google's data practices at policies.google.com/privacy.

How We Use Your Information

  • To sync your saved cards across your devices
  • To provide and improve the app's features
  • To serve ads through Google AdMob

Data Storage

Account data is stored securely using Google Firebase. We do not sell your data to third parties.

Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from within the app. Go to My Cards → scroll to Account → Delete Account. This permanently removes your account and all associated data.

Children's Privacy

Rewards Easily is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@rewardseasily.app.
