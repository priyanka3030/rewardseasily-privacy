# App Store Optimization — Rewards Easily
Copy-paste these into App Store Connect → App Information / Version Information.

---

## App Name  (30 chars max)
```
Rewards Easily – Credit Cards
```
Current name "Rewards Easily" (14 chars) wastes 16 chars of the highest-weight keyword slot.
Adding "Credit Cards" makes you findable for the most-searched term in your category.

---

## Subtitle  (30 chars max)

**Recommended:**
```
Best Card for Every Purchase
```
(28 chars) — best for conversion, speaks directly to search intent.

**Alternatives:**
```
Cashback & Rewards Optimizer
```
(28 chars) — better for keyword indexing if you drop "cashback" from the keywords field.

```
Find Your Best Rewards Card
```
(27 chars) — mirrors exactly how people search.

## Keywords Field  (100 chars exactly — paste as-is, no edits)
```
cashback,optimizer,wallet,compare,points,dining,travel,groceries,amex,chase,visa,gas,calculator,citi
```

## Full Description

```
Rewards Easily shows you which credit card earns the most cashback or points for every purchase — instantly.

Search by merchant name or pick a category: dining, groceries, gas, travel, or online shopping. Your wallet ranks automatically, with real dollar estimates so you know exactly what you're earning.

PERSONALIZED TO YOUR WALLET
Add the cards you own and Rewards Easily ranks only your cards — not a generic list. Your best card shows up first, every time.

DISCOVER CARDS WORTH ADDING
See the top-earning card for each category, even if it's not in your wallet yet. Find out if it's worth applying for before you apply.

SPECIAL OFFERS
Limited-time bonus rates on your cards appear automatically. Never miss a promotional window again.

REWARDS PROGRAMS
Bank of America Preferred Rewards and US Bank Altitude members: set your tier and watch your earn rates update across every card in your wallet.

SECURE & PRIVATE
Sign in with email to sync your wallet across devices. Enable Face ID for quick, secure access. No tracking, no selling your data.

Free to use. Works with Visa, Mastercard, Amex, and Discover cards from all major US banks — Chase, Amex, Citi, Capital One, Bank of America, Wells Fargo, and more.
```

The first two paragraphs are "above the fold" — everything visible before the "more" tap.
They cover the core value prop and the three most common search queries (cashback, credit card,
rewards) without reading like a keyword dump. The ALL CAPS section headers are a standard
App Store convention that helps users skim.

## How to Apply in App Store Connect

1. Go to App Store Connect → your app → **App Information**
2. Update **Name**: "Rewards Easily" → "Rewards Easily – Credit Cards"
3. Add **Subtitle** (currently blank): paste chosen subtitle above
4. Go to **Version Information** → scroll to **Keywords**
5. Paste the 100-char keywords string above (replace everything)
6. Update **Description**: paste the full description above
7. Save → Submit with your next build

> Note: Name, subtitle, and keyword changes go live with the next approved update.
> You do NOT need a new build just for metadata — you can submit the current build
> with updated metadata.
