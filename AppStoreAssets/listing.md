## Description (4000 chars max)

Stop leaving money on the table. Rewards Easily instantly tells you which credit card in your wallet earns the most rewards for every purchase — so you always swipe the right card.

**Know Your Best Card in Seconds**
Dining out? Booking a flight? Filling up at the pump? Just tap the category and Rewards Easily ranks every card you own by reward rate — highest earner at the top, every time.

**Your Entire Wallet, Ranked**
Add the cards you own and Rewards Easily does the math for you. See exactly how much you earn per dollar, compare your cards side by side, and know at a glance which one wins for every spending category.

**Never Miss a Limited Offer**
Credit cards run limited-time bonus offers that can triple your rewards — and most people miss them entirely. Rewards Easily surfaces active offers front and center so you always know when a card has a special boost.

**Discover Cards Worth Adding**
Don't have the best card for a category? Rewards Easily shows you the top-earning card you don't own yet — so you can make smarter decisions about which card to get next.

**Search by Merchant**
Type in Amazon, Starbucks, or any merchant and Rewards Easily maps it to the right spending category automatically. No guessing. No looking it up.

**Secure & Private**
Sign in with Face ID for instant, secure access. Your card selections are saved to your account so your wallet is always ready on any device.

**Categories Covered**
• Dining & Restaurants
• Groceries & Supermarkets
• Gas Stations
• Travel (Flights, Hotels, Rideshare)
• Online Shopping
• Everything Else

Whether you have 2 cards or 12, Rewards Easily makes sure every dollar you spend earns as much as possible. Download free and start maximizing your rewards today.
