Support

We're here to help

🃏 How do I add my credit cards?

Tap the My Cards tab at the bottom of the app. Use the search bar to find your card by name, then tap the + button to add it to your wallet.

🔍 My merchant isn't being recognized

Not every merchant is in our database yet. If a search doesn't find a match, simply pick the closest spending category manually (e.g. choose "Dining" for a restaurant).

📱 How do I sync across devices?

Create a free account by tapping Log In on the My Cards screen. Once signed in, your cards sync automatically across all your devices.

🗑️ How do I delete my account?

Go to the My Cards tab → scroll to the bottom → tap Delete Account. This permanently removes your account and all saved data.
